  - List all payments:
    - Method: GET
    - URL: http://your-domain.com/api/paiements/

  - Filter payments by student:
    - Method: GET
    -   URL: http://your-domain.com/api/paiements/?etudiant=1
    - (Replace '1' with the actual ID of the student you want to filter by)

  - Filter by date range:
    - Method: GET
    - URL: http://your-domain.com/api/paiements/?start_date=2023-01-01&end_date=2023-12-31

  - Order payments by amount (descending):
    - Method: GET
    - URL: http://your-domain.com/api/paiements/?ordering=-montant

  - Combine multiple filters:
    - Method: GET
    - URL: http://your-domain.com/api/paiements/?etudiant=1&statut_paiement=paid&ordering=-date_paiement

1. Setting the Stage:
Our school management system begins with the foundation of education. We start by creating the building blocks:
A new Niveau (Level): This could be "Freshman", "Sophomore", etc.
A new Filiere (Major): For example, "Computer Science" or "Business Administration".
A new Matiere (Subject): Such as "Introduction to Programming" or "Financial Accounting".
These elements form the structure of our educational offerings.


2. Introducing the Educator:
Next, we bring in a Professeur (Teacher). This individual is the cornerstone of our educational system. 
They have specific attributes like name, specialization, and contact details.

3. Forming the Learning Environment:
With our teacher ready, we create a Groupe (Group). 
This is where the magic of education happens. We assign our Professeur to this Groupe, 
linking the educator to a specific Niveau, Filiere, and Matiere. 
It's like creating a classroom with a purpose.

4. Welcoming the Learner:
Now, we introduce an Etudiant (Student) to our system. 
This young mind is eager to learn and grow.
 We capture their personal details and academic aspirations.

5. Joining the Educational Journey:
The final step in our initial setup is to assign our Etudiant to the Groupe we created. 
This action connects the student to their teacher, subject, and peers, setting the stage for their educational journey.

5 .The Learning Cycle Begins:
With everything in place, the cycle of learning can begin. 
The Professeur can now conduct classes, assign work, and evaluate progress.
The Etudiant attends classes, submits assignments, and grows in knowledge.

7. Tracking Progress and Finances:
As the educational process continues, we need to keep track of various aspects:
Paiements (Payments): Students or their guardians make payments for the education received.
Comissions (Commissions): Teachers receive commissions based on the payments made by their students.

8. Continuous Management:
Throughout the academic year, our system allows for:
Adding new students to groups
Creating new groups as needed
Updating student and teacher information
Managing payments and commissions
Scheduling events and classes



Create a new staff user (admin only):
POST /api/staff/register/
{
    "username": "newstaff",
    "password": "yourpassword",
    "password2": "yourpassword",
    "email": "staff@example.com",
    "first_name": "Staff",
    "last_name": "User"
}

2. Get a new access token:
POST /api/token/
{
    "username": "newstaff",
    "password": "yourpassword"
}


3. Update profile (authenticated users):
PUT /api/staff/profile/
{
    "first_name": "Updated",
    "last_name": "Name",
    "email": "newemail@example.com"
}

4. Refresh token:
POST /api/token/refresh/
{
    "refresh": "your-refresh-token"
}
